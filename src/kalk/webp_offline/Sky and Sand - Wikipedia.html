Sky and Sand

Song by Paul and Fritz Kalkbrenner

Sky and Sand is a song by producer brothers Paul and Fritz Kalkbrenner . It's a techno track with vocals. The production comes from Paul Kalkbrenner, text and vocals by Fritz Kalkbrenner. The title was recorded as the theme song of the film Berlin Calling by Hannes Stöhr . It appeared on the film's soundtrack in 2008 and was also released as a single on vinyl and CD on the BPitch Control label in 2009.

reception Edit

use Edit

Outside of the film Berlin Calling, Sky and Sand has been used by the energy supplier RWE as background music for a TV commercial since 2011 .

Charts and chart positions Edit

Sky and Sand reached the German singles charts for the first time on February 12, 2010 . On November 11, 2011, the single peaked at number 29 in its 83rd week. It is the longest time it took for a title to reach its highest position in the German charts. With 129 chart weeks , Sky and Sand is one of the longest-charting titles of all time in the German single charts . [1] In Austria , the title was in the hit parade for 69 weeks and reached its highest ranking at number 37. [2]
